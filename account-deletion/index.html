Important: Cinopsys uses Trakt.tv for authentication. Since we do not host your account data on our own servers, your account management is handled via the Trakt platform.

How to permanently delete your account

  1. Go to Trakt.tv and sign in to your profile.
  2. Navigate to Settings (found in the user menu).
  3. Scroll down to the Advanced section.
  4. Click Delete Account and follow the on-screen instructions to confirm.
What happens next?
This action is permanent. It will remove your watch history, lists, and profile data from Trakt and disconnect your access to Cinopsys.

Local Data

To remove any cached data or settings stored locally on your device, simply uninstall the Cinopsys app from your phone or tablet.
